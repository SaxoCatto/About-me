Introduction

I like cats, and I like saxophones. Hence my name. I am trying out things, from programming to exercises. I try to enjoy things that I chose. As I move on with life, I strive to be a independant, competant and reliable person. And learning many things, particularly programming, is my chosen path to achieving it.

On this page, I use a monospace grid to align text and draw diagrams. It’s generated from a simple Markdown document, and the CSS. There is Javascript, but I hope it kinda works. The page is responsive, shrinking in character-sized steps. Standard elements should just work, at least that’s the goal. It’s semantic HTML, rendered as if we were back in the Old Internet.
