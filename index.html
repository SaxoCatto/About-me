Introduction

I like cats, and I like saxophones. Hence my name. I am trying out things, from programming to exercises. I try to enjoy things that I chose. As I move on with life, I strive to be a independant, competant and reliable person. And by learning things, top down and bottoms up, do I appreciate these fleeting memories.

On this page, I use a monospace grid to align text and draw diagrams. It’s generated from a simple Markdown document (using Pandoc), and the CSS and a tiny bit of Javascript renders it on the grid. Standard elements should just work fluidly. It’s semantic HTML, made as if we were back in the 70s.

All right, but is this even a good idea? It’s a technical and creative challenge and I like the aesthetic. As long as I am passionate about it, time spent here is worthwhile. Of course, I will add more elements to this page. Maybe turn this into a main blog page.
