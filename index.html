Introduction

I like cats, and I like saxophones, hence my name. Since time flies by so fast, I try to enjoy things that I chose. As I move on with life, I strive to be a independant, competant and reliable person. And by learning things, top down and bottoms up, do I appreciate these fleeting memories.

All right, but is this even a good idea? It’s a technical and creative challenge, and I like the aesthetic. As long as I am passionate about it, time spent here is worthwhile. Of course, I will hook more pages to this. So in near future, treat this like a "About me" page.

For this website

For this page, I use a Monospace grid to align text and draw diagrams. It’s generated from a simple Markdown document (using Pandoc), and the CSS and a tiny bit of Javascript renders it on the grid. Standard elements should just work (TM). It’s semantic HTML, made as if we were back in the 70s.
